Install, configure & ship

Everything you need to get Saathi running on your WordPress site.

InstallationConnect an AI providerScan your websiteAppearance & mascotPlacement rulesActivate licenseFAQ

1 · Installation

  1. Download sathi-agentic-ai.zip (or clone the repo).
  2. WordPress → Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin → choose the zip → Install.
  3. Click Activate. A new Saathi AI menu appears.
  4. Requirements: WordPress 6.4+, PHP 8.1+. WooCommerce optional.

2 · Connect an AI provider

Go to Saathi AI → AI Providers, paste your key, click Test connection, then Save. 15 providers supported (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, OpenRouter, Groq, Ollama, custom & more). Choose a separate cheaper provider for embeddings if you like.

3 · Scan your website

Saathi AI → Knowledge → Scan website & train Saathi. It indexes products, posts, pages & CPTs and re-indexes automatically on publish.

4 · Appearance & mascot

Under Saathi AI → Chatbot set the title, greeting, accent colour, position, theme, and pick one of the 8 mascots — the widget updates instantly.

5 · Placement rules

Show everywhere, only on selected pages, or hide on selected pages. Limit by post type or to logged-in users.

6 · Activate your license

Go to Saathi AI → License, enter your SATHI-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX key, click Activate. Stored encrypted, re-checked every 24 hours.
