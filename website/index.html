Install, configure & ship

Everything you need to get Sathi running on your WordPress site.

Installation Connect an AI provider Scan your website Appearance & mascot Placement rules Activate license FAQ

1 · Installation

  1. Download sathi-agentic-ai.zip (or clone the repo).
  2. WordPress → Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin → choose the zip → Install.
  3. Click Activate. A new Sathi AI menu appears in your sidebar.
  4. Requirements: WordPress 6.4+, PHP 8.1+. WooCommerce is optional (product features auto-hide without it).

2 · Connect an AI provider

Go to Sathi AI → AI Providers. Pick any provider and paste your key:

  • Cloud: OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, DeepSeek, Mistral, Perplexity, xAI Grok, Cohere.
  • Fast/aggregator: OpenRouter, Groq, Together, Fireworks. Local: Ollama, LM Studio. Plus a custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.
  • Click Test connection to verify, then Save. Set one as default. You can also choose a separate, cheaper provider for embeddings.

3 · Scan your website

Open Sathi AI → Knowledge and click Scan website & train Sathi. It indexes your products, posts, pages and custom post types so answers come only from your content. It re-indexes automatically when you publish or update content.

4 · Appearance & mascot

Under Sathi AI → Chatbot set the title, greeting, accent colour, position, theme (light/dark/auto) and choose one of the 5 mascots. The widget updates instantly.

5 · Placement rules

Show the widget everywhere, only on selected pages, or hide it on selected pages. Limit by post type, or show it only to logged-in users.

6 · Activate your license

Go to Sathi AI → License, enter your SATHI-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX key and click Activate. The key is stored encrypted and re-checked automatically every 24 hours.

FAQ

Are my API keys safe? Yes — encrypted at rest, never sent to the browser.

Do I need WooCommerce? No. Product cards appear only if WooCommerce is active.

Which model is best on a budget? OpenRouter has excellent free models; Sathi works great with them.

Multiple sites? Each license covers a set number of domains based on your plan.
